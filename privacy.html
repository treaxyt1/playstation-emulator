Privacy Policy

Your privacy is paramount at ePSXemu. Our system is designed so that your data remains yours.

1. No Data Collection

ePSXemu does not collect personal identification information, email addresses, or ROM files. We do not have a server-side database for user content.

2. Local Storage (IndexedDB)

The files you "upload" are stored locally on your device within your browser's IndexedDB. We cannot access, view, or delete these files from our servers.

3. Analytics

We use basic, anonymized analytics to monitor website uptime and performance. This data does not include your IP address or individual gaming habits.
