The Definitive PS1 Library

01

Metal Gear Solid (1998)

Hideo Kojima's masterpiece. This game essentially invented the "Cinematic Action" genre. Its use of original hardware capabilities was nothing short of legendary.

02

Final Fantasy VII (1997)

The JRPG that brought the genre to the global stage. Cloud Strife's journey across Midgar and beyond remains one of the most emotional narratives in gaming history.

03

Castlevania: Symphony of the Night (1997)

The perfect blend of exploration, RPG elements, and pixel art. It redefined the franchise and gave birth to the 'Metroidvania' sub-genre.

04

Resident Evil 2 (1998)

The gold standard for survival horror. Capcom's sequel improved on every aspect of the original, providing multiple interwoven scenarios and terrifying atmosphere.

05

Gran Turismo 2 (1999)

With hundreds of cars and a realistic physics engine for its time, Polyphony Digital created the ultimate driving simulator that pushed the PS1 to its limits.

All titles mentioned are for preservation review only. Please ensure you own the physical disc before emulating.
