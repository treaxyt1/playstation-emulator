Where to Find PS1 ROMs

To use ePSXemu, you need game files (ROMs) in .bin format. While we do not host games, here are the most trusted sources for legal game preservation.

⚠️ Legal Warning & Disclaimer

ePSXemu does not host, distribute, or provide ROMs. We only provide links to third-party preservation websites. Users are exclusively responsible for ensuring they legally own the games they access. We strongly recommend only downloading games you physically own in your collection. Emulation is for preservation and personal archival use.

Digital Preservation Libraries

Internet Archive

A non-profit library of millions of free books, movies, and software. Internet Archive hosts legally preserved software including abandonware.

Visit archive.org

Romulation

One of the oldest and most established community ROM databases with a high focus on reliability and security.

Visit romulation.org

CDRomance

Specializes in retro computer and console game preservation with a massive database for the original PlayStation.

Visit cdromance.com

Vimm's Lair

Dedicated to nostalgia, Vimm's Lair has been a trusted sanctuary for emulators and ROMs since 1997.

Visit vimm.net

EdgeEmulation

Classic game repository with an extensive collection of PlayStation 1 titles categorized by region and year.

Visit edgeemulation.com

RomsMania

Simple and clean interface for finding retro games, featuring a massive library of PS1 titles.

Visit romsmania.cc

Official Legal Alternatives

If you prefer to support developers directly, these platforms offer official re-releases of PlayStation classics:

PlayStation Plus Premium

Sony's official subscription service includes a growing catalog of emulated PS1 classics for modern consoles.

Steam & GOG

Many PS1 legends like Resident Evil and Final Fantasy have official re-releases available for purchase on PC platforms.

Education: Dumping your own discs

The gold standard of legal emulation is creating your own .bin files from your physical discs. You can use free software like ImgBurn to create a digital backup of your collection in minutes.
