Common Questions

Need help? Check out our most frequently asked questions.

What is ePSXemu? ▼
ePSXemu is a browser-based PlayStation 1 emulator. It allows you to play classic PS1 games directly in your web browser without installing any software. We use high-performance WebAssembly technology to deliver a near-native gaming experience.
Where do I get the games? ▼
To use ePSXemu, you must provide your own game files in .bin format. We do not provide, host, or link to any copyrighted game data. You can back up your own physical discs using a PC. Check our Find ROMs page for legal preservation sites.
Is it really free? ▼
Yes, ePSXemu is 100% free and open source. We are supported by the community and retro gaming enthusiasts.
How do I save my progress? ▼
ePSXemu supports 'Save States'. You can save your exact position in the game at any time using the Save button in the control panel. These saves are stored securely in your browser's local database.
Can I use a controller? ▼
Absolutely. ePSXemu supports any standard USB or Bluetooth gamepads recognized by your browser, including Xbox and PlayStation controllers. Plug it in, and the emulator will automatically detect it.
